About the MGNREGA Program

The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA) is a landmark social security initiative by the Government of India that guarantees 100 days of wage employment every year to rural households.

The aim is not just employment, but empowerment ‚Äî to strengthen livelihoods, enhance rural infrastructure, and ensure dignity through meaningful work. From building ponds to maintaining roads, every project under MGNREGA contributes to long-term rural development.

Since its inception in 2005, MGNREGA has changed millions of lives by providing stability in uncertain times, supporting women‚Äôs participation, and nurturing the roots of India‚Äôs villages.
